AGRIREG™ FAQs

Do I need a CFIA licence under the Safe Food for Canadians Regulations?

If you import, manufacture, or export most foods, you likely need an SFCR licence and a written PCP/HACCP program. We scope this in step 1 and map the exact path.

How long do PMRA registrations take?

Timelines vary by submission. Label amendments can complete in months; new TGAI/EUP programs take longer. We provide realistic bands and critical‑path actions.

Can you help with bilingual labels and SDS?

Yes—end‑to‑end EN/FR labels and SDS with change control and printer‑ready artwork to reduce remands and reprints.

Do you work with startups and global brands?

Both. AGRIREG™ scales from single products to large, multi‑product portfolios through a vetted Canadian specialist network.
